MISTY

Misty is a Gym Leader of Cerulean City's Gym, known officially as the Cerulean Gym. She gives the Cascade Badge to Trainers who defeat her in battle. She specializes in Water-type Pokémon.

ASH

Ash Ketchum is the main character of the Pokémon anime. He is also the main character of various manga based on the anime. He is a Pokémon Trainer from Pallet Town whose goal is to become a Pokémon Master. On his journeys, he's been accompanied by many of close friends.

BROCK

Brock is the Gym Leader of Pewter City's Gym, known officially as the Pewter Gym. He gives the Boulder Badge to Trainers who defeat him in battle. He specializes in Rock-type Pokémon.
